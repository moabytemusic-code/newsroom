# RIMS News Jacking: Strategy & Intelligence
*Derived from the RIMS 9 Case Studies*

## 🎯 Winning Offer Strategy
The "Big Three" niches always perform best for broad newsjacking traffic because they appeal to almost everyone:
1. **Health** (Weight loss, pain relief, Nutra)
2. **Wealth** (Aurum AI, Personal Loans, Credit Scores, Crypto)
3. **Relationships** (Dating apps, social connection)

> [!TIP]
> **Don't over-specialize.** Specialized offers (e.g., "Learn to play the Theremin") fail because the audience is too small. Stick to "Mass Appeal" offers.

## 📈 Proven Ad Placements (RIMS Layout)
Our news page uses a specific weighting for ad clicks:
- **Sticky Ticker (Top Right):** Usually the highest-clicked link on the page (use a curiosity gap here).
- **Sidebar Native Ads:** Great for secondary "Sponsored Resource" offers.
- **In-Content Banners:** Captures users who are deeply reading the story.
- **Exit Pop-up:** Essential for capturing "bouncers" before they leave.

### Key Metrics to Watch
- **Push Notification CTR:** Aim for **0.5% – 2.0%** (Fresh news can hit **8%+**).
- **Landing Page CTR:** Aim for **1.0% – 3.0%** (Clicks from the news page to your affiliate link).
- **Conversion Rate:** High-quality "News Mode" visitors should convert between **0.5% – 1.0%**.

## 🚀 Traffic Execution (PropellerAds / RollerAds)
- **Targeting:** Focus on **Tier 1** countries (USA, Canada, Australia, New Zealand) for highest lead value.
- **Notification Type:** Use **In-Page Push (IPP)** for maximum reach across all devices, including iOS (which standard push often misses).
- **Creative Refresh:** If CTR dips, change the notification image or headline. Freshness is the #1 driver of cheap clicks.

## ⚖️ Compliance & Safety
- **Avoid Politics:** Political news often gets rejected by publishers or restricted in reach.
- **Fact-Based Headlines:** Use information gaps rather than "bait and switch." Tell them *what* happened, and use the ad to show them *how* to solve it.
- **Mandatory Disclaimer:** Always include the risk disclaimer on finance/crypto offers to pass compliance audits.
